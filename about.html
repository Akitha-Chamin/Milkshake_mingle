How it all Started

It was a hot afternoon in Colombo 07. We wanted a milkshake. Not a watery, sad, milk-flavored drink, but a THICK, soul-satisfying shake.

We couldn't find one. So, we bought a blender.

Milkshake Mingles was born out of obsession. We tested 50 types of vanilla ice cream before finding "The One." We source our fruits directly from farmers in Nuwara Eliya and Jaffna. Every cup is a labor of love.
